*Proposal: Institution-Based Donor Registration*

*What it is:*  

Add one field in the donor registration form: *“I donate as part of:”*  
Donor selects/titles their group. Example: NGO, College, Company, Family, etc.

*How it works:*  
1. *Dedicated Field*: Donor writes “Donating with NSS Jorhat College” or “Gogoi Family Tree” or “Tata Steel Unit”.
2. *Auto Tracking*: System groups all donors by that name/tag automatically.
3. *Live Dashboard*: Govt/NGO can see at a glance → “NSS Jorhat College: 340 donors. Don Bosco School Alumni: 120 donors. Gogoi Family: 28 donors.”

*Why we should do this:

**Benefit**	**Result**
**Track Impact**	Know which schools, companies, communities give most blood
**Healthy Competition**	“Don Bosco vs Jorhat College - who donates more this year?” Youth + orgs participate more
**Targeted Drives**	If “Tea Garden Workers Union” has low donors, run camp there specifically
**Recognition**	Govt can felicitate “Top 10 Institutions for Blood Donation” every year. Org pride = more donors
**Data for Planning**	Blood stock planning becomes easier. We know which institution groups to call first for A- emergency

*Simple Example:*  
Donor A registers → Selects “Donating via Rotaract Club Jorhat”  
Donor B registers → Selects “Donating via Rotaract Club Jorhat”  
System shows: “Rotaract Club Jorhat = 85 registered donors”

*In 1 line:*  
Tag every donor to their institution/family/group. Count becomes automatic. Respect + competition + better planning, all in one field.
